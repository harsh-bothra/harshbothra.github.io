Cyber Security Compliance Standards & Frameworks

Regulatory Compliance Standards

NIST SP 800-53

Official: csrc.nist.gov/SP-800-53-Rev5

Security and privacy controls for federal information systems and organizations.

ISO/IEC 27001

Official: iso.org/27001

Requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining and improving an Information Security Management System (ISMS).

ISO/IEC 27002

Official: iso.org/27002

Guidelines for organizational information security standards and information security management practices.

GDPR

Official: eur-lex.europa.eu/GDPR

Regulation on data protection and privacy in the European Union and the European Economic Area.

HIPAA

Official: hhs.gov/hipaa

Standards for electronic health care transactions, security and privacy of health data.

FISMA

Official: csrc.nist.gov/FISMA

Federal Information Security Modernization Act: framework to protect government information and operations.

FedRAMP

Official: fedramp.gov

Standardized approach to security assessment, authorization, and continuous monitoring for cloud services.

NERC CIP

Official: nerc.com/CIPStandards

Critical Infrastructure Protection standards for the bulk electric system.

PCI DSS

Official: pcisecuritystandards.org

Requirements for organizations that handle branded credit cards to protect cardholder data.

Industry Frameworks & Controls

SOC 2

Official: aicpa.org/SOC2

Trust service criteria for security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy.

CIS Controls

Official: cisecurity.org/controls

Prioritized set of actions to protect organizations and data from known cyber attack vectors.

MITRE ATT&CK

Official: attack.mitre.org

Knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques based on real-world observations.
