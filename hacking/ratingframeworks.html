Vulnerability & Risk Rating Frameworks

Scoring Systems

Common Vulnerability Scoring System (CVSS v3.1)

Official: first.org/cvss

Standardized framework to assess vulnerability severity via Base, Temporal, and Environmental metrics for consistent scoring.

Risk Rating Methodologies

OWASP Risk Rating Methodology

Official: owasp.org/OWASP_Risk_Rating_Methodology

Qualitative model combining likelihood and impact, with factors for threat agent, vulnerability, technical impact, and business impact.

DREAD

Official: docs.microsoft.com/DREAD

Scores risks based on Damage, Reproducibility, Exploitability, Affected users, and Discoverability criteria.

Threat Modeling Frameworks

STRIDE

Official: microsoft.com/STRIDE

Categorizes threats into Spoofing, Tampering, Repudiation, Information Disclosure, Denial of Service, and Elevation of Privilege.

Additional Models

FAIR

Official: fairinstitute.org

Financially driven risk analysis model quantifying loss event frequency and probable loss magnitude.

NIST SP 800-30

Official: csrc.nist.gov/SP-800-30

Risk management guide including threat & vulnerability identification, impact analysis, and risk determination steps.

ISO/IEC 31000

Official: iso.org/iso-31000

Provides principles and generic guidelines for effective risk management across enterprises and industries.
