About Me

Hey World, I am Harsh Bothra. Here a little about me :)

Harsh Bothra is currently working as a Cyber Security Analyst at Detox Technologies. Holding a bachelors degree in Computer Science & Engineering, his major interests revolve around Information Technology and Cyber Security. He is a part-time Bug Hunter on Bugcrowd (Currently ranked under Top 150 Researchers & MVP for 2020 Q1-Q2) and Synack Red Team Member. Harsh is also one of the Core Pentester at Cobalt. Harsh also blogs about his security findings through his writeups on Medium. He has authored two books on Hacking especially focusing on beginners. One of the book authored by Harsh has been previously recommneded by NITTR-Chandigarh & AICTE (Govt. of India bodies). He holds 60+ Hall of Fames from various companies. He loves to talk about various cyber security stuff and has carried out a lot of sessions related to Cyber Security, Ethical Hacking & Application Security. He is always ready to support and mentor people the best way he can.
